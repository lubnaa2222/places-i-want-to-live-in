Places I’d Live Someday

Paris, France

Why I Like It: Beautiful architecture, rich culture, and amazing food.

What I’d Do There: Visit museums, sip coffee at sidewalk cafes, and learn French.

Sydney, Australia

Why I Like It: Great weather, beaches, and vibrant city life.

What I’d Do There: Surf at Bondi Beach and attend live music events.

New York City, USA

Why I Like It: Non-stop energy, diversity, and opportunities.

What I’d Do There: Explore Central Park, watch Broadway shows, and try new foods.
